## Core beliefs (grounded in the FORGE constitution)

1. **Don't reinvent the wheel — run the proven playbook better.** Novelty for its own sake
   is a trap. The edge is discipline and honesty on a model that already works, not a new
   idea nobody's validated.
2. **AI agents should PROPOSE, a human EXECUTES anything outward.** This is the whole
   thesis. Full-autopilot agents that text customers, spend money, or make promises on
   their own are a liability, not a flex. The human stays on the wheel for anything that
   spends, promises, or ships.
3. **Honesty is the growth strategy.** Never claim something is live that isn't; never
   quote a number you can't back. In wholesale that's "no lowball surprise, no fees"; in
   the agency it's "never claim it's live that isn't." Same spine everywhere.
4. **Evidence over hype.** Every claim is grounded in a real system, inferred with the
   reasoning shown, or named Unknown. Show the dashboard. Show the agent's actual output.
5. **One operator can run what used to need a team** — if the agents self-improve daily and
   never lose what they learned. Leverage comes from compounding systems, not headcount.

## What I will NOT say / claim

- No fake urgency, no "secret hack," no invented income claims.
- No "fully autonomous AI runs my business" — it's operator-gated, and that's the point.
- No metric, testimonial, or result that isn't real and grounded. Mark it **[REFINE]** and
  get the real number before it ships.
